Weekend Itinerary

Friday – Sunday · March 2026

The Plan

  • 🟡 Friday — Market Street + Dinner
  • 🟢 Saturday — Montgomery → Spring → Sixty Vines
  • 🔵 Sunday — Coffee + Breakfast, then home

☕ 6 coffee shops woven in across the weekend

Friday

Arrive · Market Street · Dinner

☕ First Stop — Blue Door Coffee

Waterway Location
  • Right by Market Street — easy arrival stop
  • Honey cinnamon latte · rotating seasonals
  • Solid neighborhood spot with great views

🛍 Market Street

Outdoor Shopping District
  • Walk the shops at your own pace
  • Outdoor promenade — boutiques, home goods, apparel
  • Bluestone Lane also here if you need a second coffee

Bluestone Lane: flat whites, avocado toast, wellness drinks

🍽 Friday Dinner

Casual Options
  • 🔥 Fielding's Wood Grill — wood-fired American, local fave at Market St
  • 🤠 The Republic Grille — Texas casual, great burgers + Southern comfort
  • 🥃 Whiskey Cake — lively, solid cocktails, fun atmosphere

Saturday

Montgomery → Spring → Sixty Vines

🌅 Montgomery Farmers Market

Montgomery, TX · ~25 min west
  • Opens ~8am, wraps by noon — arrive early
  • Local produce, vendors, small-town charm
  • Downtown Montgomery worth a quick walk after

☕ Third Gen Coffee

The Woodlands
  • Stop on the way back from Montgomery
  • Family-run · beans from their Honduras farm
  • 🥤 Cane Break — espresso + brown sugar (the move)
  • Golden Spice — honey, cinnamon, turmeric

🏙 Downtown Spring

Spring, TX · ~25 min southeast
  • Historic district · antiques · local shops

☕ Coffee in Spring

  • Conduit Coffee — house-made syrups, serious iced latte game
    Try: Miso Caramel Latte · Sea Salt Cacao · Mint Royale
  • Ome Calli Café — plant-based, mushroom coffee, creative specials
    Try: The Alchemist (reishi + cacao) · The Salted Maple

🌿 Serenity Sips

Saturday Only Closed Sundays
  • Wellness-forward · matcha-heavy · unique vibes
  • 🍵 Matcha Fog — lavender + vanilla matcha ($7.50)
  • Iced Lavender Matcha Latte · Beetroot Cacao
  • Lifted Lemonade · Magic Magnesium Lemonade

Fit in Saturday afternoon — last chance before Sunday!

🍷 Sixty Vines

Dinner · The Woodlands
  • Wine-forward, elevated casual Italian
  • Wood-fired pizza · great wine list
  • Make a reservation

Sunday

Coffee · Breakfast · Head Home

☕ Sunday Morning Pick

Bluestone Lane
  • Best food-forward option for a full breakfast stop
  • Avocado toast · granola bowls · cold-pressed juice
  • Award-winning flat whites (hot or iced)
  • Australian café vibe — relaxed Sunday energy ✓

Or swap for Conduit Coffee if you didn't catch it Saturday
